Our Story

Gospel Church Union was born out of a desire to create a Christ-centered community where people from all walks of life can encounter God's love, grow in faith, and find purpose. What started as a small gathering of 30 believers has grown into a vibrant family of hundreds, spanning multiple nations and generations.

We believe that the Church is not a building — it's a living, breathing community of people who are passionate about following Jesus and making a difference in the world around them.

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples, building community, and transforming lives through the power of the Gospel.
